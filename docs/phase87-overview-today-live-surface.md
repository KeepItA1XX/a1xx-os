# Phase 87 Overview Today Live Surface

Current Phase: Phase 87: First Live Player Data Surfaces
Current Pass: Pass 1D: Overview Today Live Surface
Pass Type: first live player surface / read-only active

## Surface

`Account > Mission > Overview > Today` is the first player-facing surface allowed to consume the read-only player packet.

## Boundary

- The surface may show up to 3 current Today moves.
- The surface does not create new moves.
- The surface does not complete missions.
- The surface does not award XP, badges, trophies, resources, or milestones.
- The surface does not dispatch notifications.
